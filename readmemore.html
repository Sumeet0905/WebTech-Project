Le Fiestus

Jaypee University of Information Technology recently concluded its highly anticipated cultural fest "Le Fiestus". The 3-day event saw a massive turnout with electrifying performances by student artists, national-level dance and music competitions, and a vibrant display of cultural heritage.

The highlight of the fest included celebrity performances, food stalls, gaming zones, and engaging workshops. The campus came alive with color, energy, and enthusiasm, making it one of the most memorable editions of Le Fiestus yet.

Career Counseling Session

JUIT faculty members visited Mount Shivalik School to conduct an insightful career counseling session. The aim was to guide school students on various emerging academic paths, including Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, and Electronics & Communication Engineering.

Experts highlighted the integration of Artificial Intelligence in traditional fields, the rise of interdisciplinary careers, and how entrepreneurship is reshaping opportunities for students. Attendees appreciated the clarity and motivation provided through this initiative.

Great Achievement - Ideathon 2025

A proud moment for JUIT as its students were crowned national winners at the prestigious SBI College Youth Ideathon 2025 held at IIT Delhi. Competing against hundreds of innovative minds from across the country, JUIT students stood out with their project aimed at sustainable urban development.

The team received accolades from industry experts, and the project is being considered for incubation under the StartUp India initiative. This milestone sets a benchmark for innovation and excellence at the university.

Placement Success - Zscaler Winter Internship 2025

Jaypee University of Information Technology proudly announces that nine of its students have been selected by Zscaler for the Winter Internship 2025 program. The selection process was rigorous, involving multiple technical and HR rounds.

This placement reflects the high caliber of talent nurtured at JUIT and the commitment of the university to align students with industry demands. The selected interns will gain hands-on experience in cybersecurity, software development, and cloud infrastructure.
